Frequently Asked Questions

What is Manila and what am I looking at?
Manila is an opportunity market, created by Palestra, focused on advancing the human experience through connecting talented individuals to meaningful projects, grants, positions, and policy work that contribute to a better future.

Our opportunities fall into four categories:
- Bounties: Quick-impact projects with immediate compensation
- Contracts: High-value engagements for specialized expertise
- Research: Funded positions advancing key research
- Jobs: Full-time positions
How do I apply for an opportunity or post my own?
You can apply directly by clicking on a listing then clicking 'Apply'. You can create your own listings by clicking 'submit' at the top.
What are Manila Grants?
The 'grants' tab is where you can apply for grants given out by Manila by submitting a research topic or a business idea. This is not currently available.
What is the journal?
Manila Journal is where complete research will be hosted upon completion for others to view, ask questions, and contact the author(s).
Who or what is Palestra?
Manila is a tool built by Palestra to connect the people in powerful networks to opportunties that cannot be found elsewhere. Palestra believes that with proper infrastructure, a select few can achieve the impossible. To join the Palestra Network, click the link at the top of the page.
How do I recieve payment?
Bounties are paid out by their host upon proof of completion, large bounties may be held in escrow for security purposes. For jobs, payments are handled directly by employers.
How do you verify opportunities?
We have a verification process for all organizations and individuals posting opportunities. This includes checking business registration, past history, user reviews, and reaching out directly. High-value opportunities undergo additional verification.
What fees does Manila charge?
Manila charges a 5% fee on successful contract and bounty completions. Job postings are currently free for verified organizations.
Can I post opportunities from anywhere?
Yes, Manila is a global platform. However, all opportunities must comply with local laws and our platform guidelines.
How do you handle disputes?
We have a dedicated dispute resolution team. For escrow payments, funds are held until disputes are resolved. We encourage clear communication and documentation to prevent disputes.

About Manila

Manila is an opportunities marketplace focused on advancing human flourishing through technology, research, and policy work. We connect talented individuals with meaningful projects, grants, and positions that contribute to building a better future.

Our Programs

Bounties: Short-term projects with specific deliverables and immediate compensation.

Research Grants: Funding for independent researchers and small teams exploring crucial questions.

Accelerator: Support for early-stage projects and startups building sovereignty-enhancing technology.

Jobs: Full-time positions with organizations aligned with our mission.

Fellowships: Longer-term funded positions for deep research and development work.

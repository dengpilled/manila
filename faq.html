Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started on Maynila?
Create an account, complete your profile with relevant skills and experience, and start browsing opportunities. You can apply directly to posts or create your own listings.
How does payment work?
We offer secure payment processing through our platform. For contracts and bounties, funds are held in escrow until work is completed and approved. For jobs, payments are handled directly by employers.
How do you verify opportunities?
We have a verification process for all organizations posting opportunities. This includes checking business registration, past history, and user reviews. High-value opportunities undergo additional verification.
What fees does Maynila charge?
Maynila charges a 5% fee on successful contract and bounty completions. Job postings are currently free for verified organizations.
How do I report suspicious activity?
Use the "Report" button on any post or profile, or contact our support team directly at support@maynila.com. We take all reports seriously and investigate promptly.
Can I post opportunities from anywhere?
Yes, Maynila is a global platform. However, all opportunities must comply with local laws and our platform guidelines.
How do you handle disputes?
We have a dedicated dispute resolution team. For escrow payments, funds are held until disputes are resolved. We encourage clear communication and documentation to prevent disputes.
